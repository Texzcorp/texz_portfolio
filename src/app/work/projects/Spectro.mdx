## Overview

Spectro is a software designed to enhance users' desktop experience by automatically downloading and updating wallpapers based on selected themes. It allows customization through various categories, from scenic views to art styles, ensuring fresh and personalized wallpapers daily.

## Key Features

- **Automated Wallpaper Updates**: Download new images daily based on preferences.
- **Theme Customization**: Choose themes ranging from nature to modern art.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Simple setup and automation.

## Technologies Used

- **Python**: For scripting automation.
- **Pillow**: To process images.
- **Vercel**: Hosting for cloud-based features.

## Challenges and Learnings

Implementing smooth wallpaper transitions and maintaining server-side performance for image fetching were critical hurdles. Learning efficient methods for image processing at scale enhanced the software's usability.

## Outcome

Spectro brings fresh visuals to desktops with minimal user intervention, perfect for those looking to personalize their workspace effortlessly.
