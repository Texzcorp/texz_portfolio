## Overview

Départage is a party game developed for my family as a Christmas gift, designed to help distribute household chores through fun and competitive gameplay. Built with Unreal Engine 4, the game features complex ranking systems for fair task distribution, accompanied by carefully crafted visuals, animations, and seamless transitions. Players are taken on a journey through three thematic worlds: Feudal Japan, Ancient Egypt, and Medieval Europe, each with its own unique atmosphere.

## Key Features

- **Complex Ranking System**: A sophisticated architecture for tracking and distributing points based on player performance.
- **Thematic Worlds**: Travel through Feudal Japan, Ancient Egypt, and Medieval Europe, each with its own set of challenges and unique aesthetics.
- **Custom Composed Music**: Enjoy original soundtracks composed specifically for each world, enhancing the immersion and atmosphere.
- **Art Direction and Animation**: High-quality art and animation that bring each world to life, along with smooth transitions between gameplay segments.

## Technologies Used

- **Unreal Engine 4**: For building the gameplay mechanics, visual environments, and overall design.
- **Custom Ranking System**: Designed to accurately reflect player performance and fairness in task distribution.

## Challenges and Learnings

Creating a party game that is both fun and functional for a family context required careful balancing between competitive gameplay and task distribution fairness. The architecture of the ranking system, in particular, needed to be precise to ensure that no player was unfairly advantaged or disadvantaged.

## Outcome

Départage successfully fulfilled its goal as a fun and engaging way to allocate household chores. The thematic worlds, animations, and custom music provided an immersive experience that added to the overall enjoyment of the game.
