## Overview

Backdoor is a fast-paced game where players take on the role of a virus inside a computer desktop. The objective is to steal files and send them to the recycle bin while avoiding the user's mouse and the antivirus software that constantly scans for threats. It combines strategy with stealth, requiring players to stay one step ahead of the computer's defenses.

## Key Features

- **Virus Gameplay**: Control a virus with the ability to grab files and delete them.
- **Dynamic Challenges**: Evade the user's mouse and avoid detection by the antivirus system.
- **Unique Concept**: A creative twist on the stealth genre, set entirely within a computer environment.
- **Custom Art and Music**: Immersive visuals and an original soundtrack designed to enhance the tension of the gameplay.

## Technologies Used

- **Unity**: For creating the mechanics.
- **AI Systems**: Custom AI for the antivirus scans and user mouse movement to create dynamic challenges.
- **GIMP**: For designing art and animations.
- **Bosca ceoil**: For music composition
- **SFXR**: For sound design

## Challenges and Learnings

Designing a game where the player is constantly under threat required a fine balance between tension and fairness. Ensuring that the virus felt responsive while giving the antivirus and mouse realistic behaviors was a significant design challenge.

## Outcome

Backdoor offers players a unique experience, blending stealth gameplay with the digital world of a computer desktop. The combination of custom art and music creates an engaging and immersive atmosphere, making it a standout entry in the genre.
