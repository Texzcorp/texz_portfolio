## Overview

Spectro is a software designed to enhance users' desktop experience by automatically downloading and updating wallpapers based on selected themes. It allows customization through various categories, from scenic views to art styles, ensuring fresh and personalized wallpapers daily.

## Key Features

- **Automated Wallpaper Updates**: Automatically download and update new images daily based on preferences.
- **Theme Customization**: Choose themes from a variety of categories (featuring a nsfw checkbox to filter out adult content if desired).
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Easy to use.

## Technologies Used

- **Python**: For scripting automation.
- **Pillow**: To process images.

## Challenges and Learnings

The main challenge was developing innovative methods to filter and curate images from web searches. We had to create sophisticated algorithms to evaluate image quality, aspect ratios, and content relevance while maintaining a diverse selection. This involved implementing multiple filtering layers: resolution checks, composition analysis, and theme matching to ensure only high-quality, desktop-appropriate images were selected, while avoiding repetitive or unsuitable content.

## Outcome

Spectro brings fresh visuals to desktops with minimal user intervention, perfect for those looking to personalize their workspace effortlessly.
